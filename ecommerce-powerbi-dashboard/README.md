# 📊 E-commerce PowerBI Dashboard

## Overview
Transformed transactional and logistical data from the Olist e-commerce platform into an interactive Power BI dashboard to optimise revenue and improve customer satisfaction.

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python** – data cleaning and preprocessing
- **Excel** – data exploration and formatting
- **Power BI** – dashboard design and visualisation

## Key Insights
- Revenue driver analysis across product categories
- Price elasticity modelling to identify optimal pricing strategies
- Impact of delivery times on customer repurchase behaviour

## Results
An interactive dashboard providing actionable business insights for decision-makers, enabling data-driven strategies to maximise revenue and customer retention.
